How SnapWOD works

The goal is simple: less time programming timers, more time training. SnapWOD removes the friction between reading a workout and starting your first rep.

1. Capture your workout

Point your camera at the whiteboard, upload a screenshot from your gym’s app or paste text from your programming notes. SnapWOD reads the workout exactly how it’s written.

2. Let AI build the timer

With Premium, SnapWOD parses the workout into structured timer blocks: parts, rounds, work/rest, time caps and labels. You can tweak anything before you hit start.

3. Hit start and just train

Clear numbers, distinct transitions and simple controls mean you never wonder what’s next. SnapWOD keeps the clock honest while you focus on effort, pacing and execution.

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about how SnapWOD works, what’s free and what’s included with Premium.

Is SnapWOD free?
SnapWOD is free to download and use as a powerful workout timer, including offline use for running timers. There’s an optional Premium upgrade that unlocks full access to AI workout parsing and sharing workflows.
Can I share workouts or timers from SnapWOD?
Yes. Once you’ve created or parsed a workout, you can share it with others so they can follow the same structure. It’s ideal for sending sessions to training partners or athletes you coach.
What workouts can SnapWOD handle?
SnapWOD is designed for CrossFit, HYROX, hybrid and functional fitness training. It supports EMOMs, AMRAPs, interval blocks, Tabata, For Time pieces, HERO workouts, chippers, hybrid circuits and complex multi-part sessions.
How does AI workout parsing work?
With Premium enabled, you can snap a photo of a whiteboard, upload a screenshot or paste text. SnapWOD’s AI reads the workout, identifies each part and automatically builds timer blocks with durations, rounds, rest periods and labels. You can review and edit everything before starting the timer.
Does SnapWOD work offline?
Yes. Once your workouts and timers are set up, you can build and run timers offline — perfect for training outdoors, when travelling, or in gyms with poor signal.
Do I need to be a coach to use SnapWOD?
Not at all. SnapWOD is built for individuals and coaches. If you follow programming from a box, HYROX plan or online coach, you can use SnapWOD to turn that programming into structured timers without manual setup.
Which devices does SnapWOD support?
SnapWOD is currently available for iPhone running iOS 16.0 or later. Support for additional platforms may be explored in the future.

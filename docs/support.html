SnapWOD — Support

Last updated: 2025-11-16

This page provides basic help for the SnapWOD app, including common questions, troubleshooting steps, and how to contact us for support.

Getting Started

  • Scan a workout: Open the Scan tab, pick a screenshot or take a photo, then tap Load Workout.
  • Paste a workout: Open the Paste tab, paste or type your workout text, and tap Read Offline or Read with AI.
  • Run a timer: Once a workout is loaded, go to the Timer tab to run it with sounds and haptics.
  • Save workouts: Use the save options in the Timer screen to add workouts to your Library.

Common Questions

Purchases & Premium

  • What does Premium include? Premium unlocks AI reading and higher monthly limits on OCR / AI usage.
  • How do I subscribe? Open the Account tab in the app and tap the Upgrade to Premium button.
  • How do I restore purchases? On the Account tab, tap Restore purchases. This will refresh your entitlements from the App Store.
  • How do I cancel? Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or use the App Store subscription settings.

Account & Sign‑In

  • I forgot my password. On the Account tab, use the email sign‑in section and tap Forgot password to send a reset link.
  • I need to verify my email. If prompted, tap Resend email in the app. After verifying, tap I verified to refresh your account status.

OCR, AI, and Timers

  • No text was detected from my image. Try cropping to just the workout area, use a clearer screenshot or photo, and ensure the text is large enough to read.
  • AI plan extraction failed. You can still use the offline parser via Read Offline. If failures persist, you may be over a usage limit or temporarily offline.
  • Timer sounds are not playing. Make sure Timer sounds are enabled in the Account > Preferences section, and that your device is not muted or in Focus mode.

Troubleshooting Steps

  • Ensure you are on the latest version of SnapWOD from the App Store.
  • Check your internet connection if AI or cloud OCR is not working.
  • If Premium does not show as active, try Restore purchases from the Account tab.
  • If the app is unresponsive, force‑quit the app and reopen it.
  • As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app (this will remove local workout data stored only on your device).
